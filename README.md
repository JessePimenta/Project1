    <article class="markdown-body entry-content" itemprop="mainContentOfPage"><h1><a id="user-content-lightspeed" class="anchor" href="#lightspeed" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Lightspeed</h1>

<p>The objective of this game is to see who, p1 or p2, can hit more light-dots on the 60-dot grid in a span of 30 seconds. The dots will be randomly generated, and will have color combinations you can hit for extra points. Demonstrate your agility, and how cool you are.</p>

<hr>

<h2><a id="user-content-1-or-2-player-mode" class="anchor" href="#1-or-2-player-mode" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>1 or 2 Player Mode</h2>

<p>This game is ideal for 1 or 2 players, just depends on whether you want to practice your own agility or test it against others! (don't be a loner)</p>

<hr>

<h2><a id="user-content-game-extras" class="anchor" href="#game-extras" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Game Extras</h2>

<blockquote>
<ul>
<li>If you hit a special bonus light, your time will be extended by 15 seconds.</li>
<li>Hitting certain colors in a row will give you bonus points.</li>
<li>Hitting the Dot of Death will decrease your time by 10 seconds.</li>
<li>If you manage to hit over 40 dots in 30 seconds your time will be extended by 10 seconds.</li>
</ul>
</blockquote>

<hr>

<h2><a id="user-content-visual-representation" class="anchor" href="#visual-representation" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Visual Representation</h2>

Instructions:

The objective of this game is to see who, p1 or p2,
can hit more light-dots on the 60-dot grid in a span of 30 seconds.
The dots will be randomly generated, and will have color combinations you can hit for extra points.
Demonstrate your agility, and how cool you are.

  • 1 point per hit, minus 1 point per miss.
